PiggyLog

A money diary for kids,
built for families.

PiggyLog is an offline-first allowance and spending tracker for kids ages 8 and up. Money is split into three buckets — Spend, Save, Give — so kids see where every dollar goes. Parents stay in the loop without taking over.

Coming soon to Amazon Appstore & Google Play

Spend with intent

Every purchase lands in a bucket. Kids see what they have to spend before they spend it.

Save toward a goal

Kids pick what they're saving for — a Lego set, a bike — and watch the bar fill. We celebrate every milestone.

Give back

The Give bucket teaches generosity from the start. A portion of every earning is theirs to share — their choice where.

Privacy by design

  • Offline-only. Nothing leaves the device. We don't have servers in v1.
  • No ads, no trackers. No third-party analytics. No attribution SDKs. Nothing.
  • COPPA-aware. Built for kids 8+ with parental setup, easy deletion, no social features.
  • Open about it. The full policy is plain English, no legalese.
